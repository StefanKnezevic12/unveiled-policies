Unveiled - Archetypal Astrology

You can delete your Unveiled account at any time directly from within the app. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Unveiled app
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Tap the Settings icon (top right)
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your account credentials (email and encrypted password)
  • All birth chart profiles you created (birth date, time, and location)
  • Your archetype profile and rankings
  • Saved articles and bookmarked content
  • Saved beliefs and reflections
  • Support chat messages
  • App preferences and settings
  • Downloaded meditation data

What Will NOT Be Deleted

The following are managed by third parties and are not affected by account deletion:

  • Active subscriptions — You must cancel your subscription separately through Apple App Store or Google Play Store settings before deleting your account. Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription billing.
  • Payment history — Transaction records are retained by Apple or Google per their policies.
Important: Account deletion is irreversible. All your data, including birth chart profiles for yourself and others, will be permanently erased. If you wish to use Unveiled again in the future, you will need to create a new account and re-enter all information.

Can't Access the App?

If you are unable to access the app and would like to request account deletion, please email us at unveiledastro@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 days.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, please contact us at unveiledastro@gmail.com.
